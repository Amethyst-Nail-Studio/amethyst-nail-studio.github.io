Nail Health

One of the things I try to teach people is the importance of keeping your natural nails healthy. Hand and feet health is critical to a person's overall health and often can be the first sign something is amiss. Things like pedicures and manicures that involve massage are excellent for relaxing tight muscles and promoting blood circulation. Furthermore, Nail enhancements should never compromise the integrity of a natural nail. With proper application, upkeep, and removal, there should not be any damage.

Cleanliness

Sanitation is of great importance. The state has set sanitation requirements, and I go beyond those. All metal implements are scrubbed in hot soapy water, soaked in a hospital-grade disinfectant, and sterilized with a high heat machine. I scrub and soak brushes, files, and hard plastic items in disinfectant. I spray all chairs, tables, curing lamps, and other touchable surfaces with hospital-grade disinfectants. Things like toe separators, flip flops, single-use files, desk mats, pedicure liners, orange sticks are all thrown away. Also, I wear a mask to protect you, and I ask you to do the same for me.
